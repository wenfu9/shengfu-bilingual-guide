Shengfu Temple Deity & Culture Guide

福德正神 (Lord Earth God)

Fu De Zheng Shen

**福德正神**，俗稱**伯公**或**土地公**，是華人信仰中最普遍且親近百姓的神祇。

**Fu De Zheng Shen (Lord Earth God)** is the most common and accessible deity in Chinese folk religion, affectionately known as **Bo Gong** (Grandpa) or **Tu Di Gong** (Local Earth Deity).

祂主要職責是**守護土地**、**庇佑農作豐收**與**地方平安**。同時，由於土地能生財，祂也被視為**財神**，主掌財富與事業興旺。

His primary duty is to **protect the land**, ensure **bountiful harvests**, and maintain **local peace**. Due to his close connection with the earth's resources, he is also widely revered as a **God of Wealth**, overseeing prosperity and business success.

天上聖母 (Goddess of the Sea)

Mazu / Tian Shang Sheng Mu

**天上聖母**，俗稱**媽祖**或**天后**，是華人信仰中最主要且影響力最大的**海神**。

**Tian Shang Sheng Mu (Mazu / Goddess of the Sea)** is the principal and most influential **Goddess of the Sea** in Chinese folk religion.

祂的歷史原型是宋代福建的**林默娘**。媽祖因具有神力，常在海上風浪中救助遇難的漁民和船隻，因而被尊奉為海上的守護神。

Her historical figure is **Lin Mo-niang** from the Song Dynasty in Fujian. Mazu is revered as the guardian of the seas for her divine power in rescuing distressed seafarers and ships during storms.

文昌帝君 (God of Culture and Literature)

Emperor Wenchang (Wenchang Dijun)

**文昌帝君**，是華人信仰中主掌**功名利祿**、**考試運途**和**文學寫作**的神祇。

**Emperor Wenchang (Wenchang Dijun)** is the deity in Chinese folk religion who governs **scholarly success**, **examination fortune**, and **literary writing**.

祂專門**庇佑學子**在各項考試中順利通過，金榜題名，並啟發文人靈感，創作優秀作品。

He specifically **blesses students** to succeed in various examinations and inspires scholars to create excellent literary works.
